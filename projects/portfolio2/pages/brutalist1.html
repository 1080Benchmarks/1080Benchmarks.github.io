Brutalist #1

This was my first attempt at brutalist design, a form of design purposely made to look and function poorly. Going in, I always associated brutalism with websites you might find from the late 90's or early 2000's. Because of this association I deicded to use a VCR font and brash colors. I also didn't want to overcomplicate the site so I made it one page. You can click on any of the sections and with a little javascript the div appears. You can also stack each page just to make it look worse.
