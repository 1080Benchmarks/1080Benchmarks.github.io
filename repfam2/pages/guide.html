History

Inspired by the streetwear replica community, r/fashionreps I decided I would create a website where anyone could find the latest hypebeast, techwear or core clothing items. I made it my goal to do this is an accessible way that gives credit to the clothing, and its design.

Disclaimer

Of course, many items displayed on RepFam are replica items from major brands, therefore it is important to understand this before buying anything because you are taking away business from companies and are at risk of getting your items confiscated through customs.

Content

It is RepFam's main goal to keep people up to date with the latest streetwear, therefore the site is updated everyday with clothing from various sources (e.g. Taobao, reddit, cnfashionbuy). All content is uploaded by RepFam itself but you can contact us to recommend items fitting for our site.
